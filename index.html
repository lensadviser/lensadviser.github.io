Best lenses for landscape photography

Landscape lenses are specialized lenses designed for capturing wide vistas and expansive landscapes. They typically have a wide angle of view and a shorter focal length, allowing photographers to capture more of the scene in a single shot. Good landscape lenses also have excellent image quality, with sharpness and minimal distortion across the frame.

Before buying, consider these key elements when searching for the top landscape lenses that will work for you :

  • Wide Angle of View : A lens with a wide angle of view, typically between 14mm and 35mm, is essential for capturing expansive landscapes and wide vistas.
  • Aperture : A wide maximum aperture, typically f/2.8 or lower, allows more light into the camera and allows for a shallow depth of field, creating more artistic and visually appealing images.
  • Image Quality : A lens with excellent image quality, sharpness, and minimal distortion is crucial for capturing fine details in landscapes, such as textures and patterns in rocks or foliage, and for accurate color rendition and contrast.
  • Compatibility : The lens should be compatible with the camera system being used, and its size and weight should be suitable for use in the field, especially when hiking or traveling to remote locations.
