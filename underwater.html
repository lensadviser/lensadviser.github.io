Best lenses for Underwater photography

Underwater lenses are designed to work specifically in the challenging conditions of underwater photography. They are typically waterproof and have special coatings to help reduce distortion and color aberrations caused by the refractive properties of water. These lenses are essential for capturing the beauty of the underwater world, whether for scientific or artistic purposes.

Before buying, Check the main elements required for an underwater lens :

  • Waterproofing : The lens must be fully waterproof and capable of functioning properly in a submerged environment.
  • Focal length : Depending on the subject matter, a wide-angle or macro lens may be best for capturing underwater images.
  • Image stabilization : Water currents and movement can cause blurring, so having image stabilization is important.
  • Lens coatings : Look for lenses with anti-reflective coatings to minimize glare and ghosting caused by water's refractive properties.

To choose an underwater lens, consider the type of photography you want to do and the conditions of the underwater environment. Choose a lens with the appropriate focal length and make sure it's compatible with your camera. Look for lenses with underwater-specific features, such as a flat port or anti-reflective coatings.
